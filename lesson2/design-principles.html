3 Awesome design principles to use in your website

Published: Sep 24, 2021

1. Visual Hierarchy

Apple

Go to website

Visual Hierarchy is one of the most important principles behind good design. It’s the order in which the human eye perceives what it sees. It doesn’t come only from size or shape. Apple makes the “Buy” call-to-action button more prominent by using color. That is an example of using Visual Hierarchy.

2. Fitt`s Law

Airbnb

Go to website

Fitt’s law says that the time required to move to a target, for example, a button, is a function of the distance to the target and the size of the target. In other words, the bigger an object and the closer it is, the easier it is to use. Airbnb did a great job with this “Reserve” button. Note that It’s near to the fingers and is big enough to be reached easily.

3. Hick's Law

Amazon

Go to website

Hick’s Law says that every additional choice increases the time required to make a decision. The more options a user has on your website, the more difficult it is to use. To make a better web design, focus on eliminating distracting options throughout the design process. Amazon did a great job using filters to limit the choices that will make the clients reach and buy the product they want faster.
